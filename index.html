Park Bo Gum

Park Bo-gum is a South Korean actor, singer, and musician. Known for his performances in film and television across various genres, he is the youngest artist to be named Gallup Korea's Television Actor of the Year and the first and only actor to ever top Forbes Korea Power Celebrity list.
